About the Conference

This interdisciplinary conference, organized by the Sapienza School of Advanced Studies (SSAS), explores the frontiers of quantum technology, materials science, and nanoscale engineering. Taking place at Sapienza Università di Roma (Aula Amaldi), the event spans 2D materials, microscopy, superconductors, quantum optics, reticular chemistry (MOFs), nanofluidics, and nanomedicine.

Targeting Master’s students, PhD candidates, and researchers, the schedule alternates comprehensive keynote sessions by international experts with parallel, hands-on laboratory workshops.
