Vision & Scope

Bridging Nanotechnology, Materials Science & Medical Innovation

Targeting Master’s students, PhD candidates, and active researchers, this conference is calibrated to maintain rigorous technical depth while ensuring accessibility across scientific disciplines.

Building upon the highly successful format implemented during our previous conference on AI and Neuroscience, the program alternates 40-minute comprehensive keynote sessions by international experts with dedicated parallel laboratory workshops.

2D Materials & Graphene Aberration-Corrected Microscopy Terahertz & Quantum Optics Topological Superconductors Reticular Chemistry (MOFs) Nanofluidics & Nanomedicine Cultural Heritage Conservation

Why Attend?

Direct Lab Access

Gain practical experience in cutting-edge AFM microscopy, nanomedicine formulation, and plasmonic simulations.

Structured Networking

Engage directly with speakers during dedicated Q&A sessions, organized coffee intervals, lunches, and the scientific aperitif.

Prestige & Excellence

Hosted at Sapienza University's historic Aula Amaldi, bringing together top minds across Europe and Japan.

Wolf Prize Winner (2011)

Maximilian Haider

CEOS GmbH & KIT, Germany

Single-Atom Imaging

Kazu Suenaga

Osaka University & AIST, Japan

World pioneer in single-atom imaging and electron energy-loss spectroscopy (EELS) in low-dimensional nanomaterials and nanotubes.

THz Laser Inventor

Alessandro Tredicucci

Università di Pisa & NEST - CNR-NANO, Italy

Pioneer in terahertz photonics and polaritonics; inventor of the first Terahertz Quantum Cascade Laser opening new spectral horizons.

Spinoza Prize Winner (1999)

Carlo Beenakker

Leiden University, The Netherlands

Legendary theoretical physicist leading seminal discoveries in mesoscopic physics, quantum transport, and Majorana fermions for quantum computing.

Graphene Pioneer

Camilla Coletti

IIT & CNI@NEST, Italy

Coordinator of Center for Nanotechnology Innovation & IIT Graphene Labs. Pioneer in scalable CVD synthesis of pristine 2D heterostructures.

Nanorestoration

Gabriella Di Carlo

ISMN-CNR / Sapienza University, Italy

Leading expert in surface chemistry and eco-friendly nanostructured coatings applied to cultural heritage conservation and corrosion prevention.

Neural Interfaces

Francesco De Angelis

Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT), Italy

Pioneer in 3D plasmonic nanostructures, advanced optical biosensors, and single-molecule neuro-technologies for biomedical breakthroughs.

Molecular Spintronics

Giulia Serrano

University of Florence, Italy

Innovator in surface science and molecular magnetism, controlling quantum spin states on surfaces for next-gen quantum devices.
