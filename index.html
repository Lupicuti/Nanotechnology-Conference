About the Conference

This interdisciplinary conference, organized by the Sapienza School of Advanced Studies (SSAS), explores the frontiers of quantum technology, materials science, and nanoscale engineering. Spanning 2D materials, microscopy, superconductors, quantum optics, reticular chemistry (MOFs), nanofluidics, and nanomedicine.
