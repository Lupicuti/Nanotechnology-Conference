Call for Abstracts • PhDs & Young Researchers

Scientific Poster Submission & Registration

Join an international cohort of PhD candidates, postdocs, and early-career investigators presenting pioneering discoveries at the Sapienza School of Advanced Studies.

Why Submit Your Research?

The SSAS Conference Poster Session is specifically designed to give PhD students, postdocs, and early-career investigators an active platform to showcase their breakthroughs, receive constructive feedback, and establish collaborations across nanoscience and advanced engineering.

Direct Scientific Networking

Engage directly during the dedicated coffee & networking sessions with plenary speakers including Prof. Beenakker, Prof. Suenaga, Prof. Haider, and top Italian researchers.

Peer Discussion & Visibility

Present your research methodology and preliminary or published findings directly to the interdisciplinary academic community and conference attendees during open gallery sessions.

Format & Technical Setup

Posters should be printed in standard vertical A0 format (84.1 × 118.9 cm). Display stands, mounting panels, and materials will be provided on-site at the Aula Amaldi foyer.

Abstract Submission Deadline: July 15, 2026
Early submissions are recommended due to limited exhibition panel slots.
